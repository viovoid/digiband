< Game Result Screen >
     Here you will be presented with the details for this stage. The grade is much similar to what you would get in school, A-D based on a percentage of greats and perfects. F for failing the song. S, SS and SSS for a percentage of Perfects over greats. It is possible to move on to the next song and still receive an F grade. Game score is the over score for this stage and all concurrent stages. Bonus points are obtained each round based on your max combo. A full combo will give you even more bonus points. The score on the bottom is the overall score for this stage.
